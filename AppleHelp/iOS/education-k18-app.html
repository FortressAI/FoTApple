Core Philosophy

Aristotelian Virtue Framework

Unlike other educational platforms that focus solely on academic achievement, FoT Education K-18 integrates character development through Aristotle's cardinal virtues:

The Four Cardinal Virtues:

1. Justice (δικαιοσύνη - Dikaiosyne) "Giving each person their due"

  • Fair treatment of peers
  • Following rules and norms
  • Honesty in assessments
  • Respect for others' work
  • Sharing resources equitably

2. Temperance (σωφροσύνη - Sophrosyne) "Moderation and self-control"

  • Self-regulation of behavior
  • Managing frustration with difficult material
  • Balanced approach to work and play
  • Patience with learning process
  • Delayed gratification

3. Prudence (φρόνησις - Phronesis) "Practical wisdom in decision-making"

  • Strategic learning choices
  • Time management
  • Setting realistic goals
  • Seeking help when needed
  • Planning and preparation

4. Fortitude (ανδρεία - Andreia) "Courage and perseverance"

  • Persistence through challenges
  • Willingness to try difficult problems
  • Resilience after setbacks
  • Speaking up in class
  • Taking academic risks

Why This Matters:

Research shows that character development predicts long-term success more reliably than test scores alone. FoT Education K-18 tracks both academic mastery and virtue development, giving educators and parents a complete picture of student growth.

3. Assessment & Mastery Tracking

Standards-Based Grading with Mastery Levels

FoT Education K-18 uses standards-based grading, tracking student mastery of specific skills rather than averaging grades.

Mastery Levels: - 5 - Exceeding: Demonstrates advanced understanding beyond grade level - 4 - Proficient: Fully meets grade-level standard - 3 - Approaching: Developing understanding, nearly at standard - 2 - Beginning: Early development, needs significant support - 1 - Not Yet: Has not yet demonstrated understanding

Benefits: - More accurate reflection of student knowledge - Identifies specific skills needing support - Allows re-assessment (growth mindset) - Clear communication to parents - Standards-aligned reporting

5. IEP/504 Accommodations Support

Seamless Accommodation Management

Students with Individualized Education Programs (IEPs) or 504 Plans require specific accommodations. FoT Education K-18 makes this seamless.

Supported Accommodations: - Extended time (1.25x, 1.5x, 2x, custom) - Reduced distractions (simplified interface) - Text-to-speech for reading - Speech-to-text for writing - Frequent breaks (reminders) - Preferential seating (classroom management) - Modified assignments (different version) - Assistive technology integration - Behavioral supports (visual schedules)

Automatic Application: Once accommodations are configured in student profile, they apply automatically to all assignments and assessments—no need for teacher to remember or manually adjust.
