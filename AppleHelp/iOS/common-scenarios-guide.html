Workflow:

Every morning at 8 AM:
1. Take BP with home monitor: 128/82
2. Open Personal Health app
3. Say: "Hey Siri, record my vitals"
4. Siri: "What are your vital signs?"
5. Say: "Blood pressure 128 over 82, heart rate 72"
6. Siri: "Vitals recorded. Receipt ID: A3F2B91C"

Result:
- Cryptographically timestamped entry
- Can't claim she didn't monitor
- Builds 30-day trend
- Export for doctor shows compliance

After 30 days:

Before doctor appointment:
→ "Hey Siri, summarize my health for the past month"
→ Receives: Average BP: 126/80 (improved from 140/90)
→ Export PDF report with cryptographic receipts
→ Doctor sees: Medication is working, patient compliant
→ Prevents unnecessary medication increase

Value: - Objective evidence of improvement - Proves medication adherence - Prevents over-medication - Empowers patient in care decisions
