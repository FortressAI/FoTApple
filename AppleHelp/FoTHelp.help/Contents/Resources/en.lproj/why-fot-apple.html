The Problem

Professional AI Needs Trust, Not Just Intelligence

Current AI solutions (ChatGPT, Watson, etc.) share fatal flaws for professional use:

1. No Proof of Correctness - Doctors can't prove their AI recommendation in malpractice cases - Lawyers can't verify AI legal analysis for bar compliance - Teachers can't document AI assessment decisions for IEP meetings

2. Regulatory Non-Compliance - ChatGPT explicitly disclaims medical/legal advice - No HIPAA/FERPA compliance path - No FDA clearance pathway for medical use

3. Black Box Decision-Making - No audit trail for reasoning - Can't verify sources or logic - Regulatory bodies reject unexplainable AI

4. Privacy Concerns - PHI/PII sent to cloud providers - Data used for training without consent - No device-local processing option

Result: Professionals can't use AI for critical decisions, despite desperate need for assistance.
